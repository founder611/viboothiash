Our Story

Sacred Since
2025

Ecomonks Sacred Vibhuti (also known as Bhasma or Tiruniru) is the holy ash central to Hindu worship, prepared in the traditional manner by burning dried cow dung, sacred woods and herbs during Vedic fire ceremonies, or homas.

Carrying the purity of these ancient rituals, our finely sieved Vibhuti is ideal for daily tilak, pooja, yajna and meditation — with no artificial additives, exactly as temple tradition has prescribed for generations.
